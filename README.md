Project 1 — Equity Risk & Return Analysis
Analyzed JPM, GS, MS, BAC and C using live yfinance data from 2022 to present. Computed annualized returns, volatility, Sharpe ratios and max drawdown. GS led on risk-adjusted returns (Sharpe: 0.66); BAC lagged (Sharpe: 0.06). Four-panel dashboard: cumulative returns, rolling 30-day volatility and risk/return scatter.
Project 2 — US Treasury Yield Curve Analysis
Pulled live Treasury yield data across 9 maturities (1M–30Y) from FRED. Visualized the curve at three macro snapshots: pre-hike (Jan 2022), peak hikes (Oct 2023) and present. The 2s10s spread inverted for 700+ days — the longest since the 1980s, peaking at -1.0% — and has since normalized.
Project 3 — Commodity & Macro Correlation Analysis
Analyzed WTI Oil, Gold, Copper and Natural Gas futures from 2020 to present against the S&P 500 and USD Index. Gold and Copper showed -0.39 inverse correlation. 3-month momentum: WTI +74.3% on geopolitical supply pressure, Copper +13.6% on AI and electrification demand.
