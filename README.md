Project 1 — Equity Risk & Return Analysis (volatility_analysis.py)
Pulled live price data for JPM, GS, MS, BAC and C from 2022 to present using yfinance. Computed annualized returns, annualized volatility, Sharpe ratios and max drawdown. Key finding: GS was the strongest risk-adjusted performer at 0.66 Sharpe, BAC the weakest at 0.06 due to consumer banking exposure during the rate cycle. Four-panel dashboard including cumulative returns, rolling 30-day volatility and a risk/return scatter.
Project 2 — US Treasury Yield Curve Analysis (yield_curve_analysis.py)
Pulled live Treasury yield data across 9 maturities (1M to 30Y) directly from the Fed's FRED database using your API key. Visualized the yield curve at three macro snapshots — pre-hike Jan 2022, peak hikes Oct 2023 and today. Modeled the 2s10s spread as a recession signal — the inversion lasted over 700 days, the longest since the 1980s, peaking at -1.0%, and has since normalized into positive territory.
Project 3 — Commodity & Macro Correlation Analysis (commodity_analysis.py)
Pulled futures data for WTI Oil, Gold, Copper and Natural Gas from 2020 to present. Computed a full cross-asset correlation matrix against the S&P 500 and USD Index. Key finding: Gold and Copper showed a -0.39 inverse correlation — Gold as safe haven, Copper as industrial demand proxy. 3-month momentum showed WTI Oil at +74.3% driven by geopolitical tensions and Copper at +13.6% from AI data center and electrification demand.
